Additional Information

Cake orders can be made including choices from the above flavors. Flavors not on the menu may be requested, but may not be approved. Design requests must be included in the order. Most design requests are typically accepted, but if they seem extreme, a team member will help you to troubleshoot more achievable ideas. Prices vary based on the size, flavor, and design of the cake.
