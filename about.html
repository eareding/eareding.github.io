Our Business

We at Olive Butter Bakery pride ourselves on our small batch and high-quality baked goods. As such, we take on only a small number of orders each month in order to preserve this purpose. Each cake, cookie, and pastry is made with great care and hard work. We hope you enjoy!

Our Founder

Ella has had a love for baking her entire life. She has always dreamnt of starting her own small businesss, and now is that time! Ella grew up in Buffalo, and she is currently a student at the University of Wisconsin Madison. While Ella does oversee bakery operations the majority of the time, she is grateful to her amazing team for managing the monthly baking when she is unable to be there due to school!
